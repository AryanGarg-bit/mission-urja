Why We Started Mission Urja

The world is facing an unprecedented energy crisis, and climate change is accelerating. Mission Urja was created to inspire individuals to take meaningful action in conserving energy and reducing carbon emissions. Our goal is to educate, track, and reward sustainable choices while making environmental responsibility accessible to all.

What This Website Contains

Energy Department: Helps users save energy.

CO₂ Department: Provides tips on reducing energy waste and increasing efficiency.

Leaderboard: Displays the top users who have saved the most energy and reduced CO₂, motivating others to compete.

Articles: Features expert-written articles on energy conservation and CO₂ reduction.

Contact Us: Allows users to reach out for support, feedback, or collaboration opportunities.

Contest and Prizes

Mission Urja hosts an exciting competition every three months, encouraging users to reduce their energy consumption and adopt eco-friendly practices. Participants are ranked on a leaderboard based on their energy-saving contributions and less CO₂ emission.

The top 10 users on the leaderboard will receive exclusive prizes, including:

  • First Prize: ₹10,000 and a Certificate of Excellence
  • Second Prize: ₹7,500 and a Certificate of Achievement
  • Third Prize: ₹5,000 and a Certificate of Recognition
  • Fourth to Tenth Prize: ₹2,000 each and Certificates of Participation

Join the challenge, save energy, and win amazing rewards!
